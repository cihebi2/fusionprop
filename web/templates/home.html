Welcome to FusionProp

Rapid & Accurate Structure-Free Protein Property Prediction

FusionProp is a cutting-edge web server designed to accelerate research in biotechnology and pharmaceutical development by providing fast and reliable predictions for key protein properties. Leveraging the power of deep learning, FusionProp predicts protein solubility, thermostability, and toxicity simultaneously, directly from amino acid sequences.

Our novel approach utilizes an optimized weighted fusion strategy, combining deep embeddings from state-of-the-art protein language models (ESM2-650M and ESMC-600M). Crucially, FusionProp operates structure-free, eliminating the computational bottleneck associated with 3D structure prediction.
